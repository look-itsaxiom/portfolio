Open Workshop gives every Claude Code session a persistent, self-growing studio. Projects get tracked with milestones and context that survives across sessions. Work gets dispatched to specialized departments with their own knowledge bases. External AI engines (Codex, Gemini, anything with a CLI) get routed appropriate tasks to save tokens. And every piece of work links to a milestone so you can track ROI.

It ships with only one department — R&D — which acts as a stem cell. When you need engineering, design, or testing specialists, R&D researches the domain and builds the department for you. The workshop grows to match your work instead of shipping a one-size-fits-all template.

Extracted from Dream Factory, the personal studio that manages 5+ creative projects from a single terminal.
